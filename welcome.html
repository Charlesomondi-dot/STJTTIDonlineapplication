‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply?

We welcome all students aged 16 and above, including those with no disabilities, deaf/hearing impaired individuals, and those who are physically incapacitated. Applicants should have completed KCPE, KCSE, or hold a diploma or degree.

How much does it cost?

For fee information, please contact us directly using the contact details below.

How long will I hear about the status of my application?

We will review your application and contact you within 7 business days.

What support is available for deaf students?

We provide sign language interpreters, visual alerts, captioning, and modified learning environments tailored to individual needs.

Can I apply for multiple programmes?

Yes, you can submit separate applications for different programmes. Each will have its own reference number.

Is accommodation available?

Please contact the admissions office for information about hostel facilities.
